Who We Are

We are a group that sells premium Euro prop bills. We work by sourcing counterfeit bills in bulk from large private groups, allowing us to resell at competitive prices while maintaining excellent quality.

Our Quality

Our Euro bills are "S Quality" - they can easily be used for film productions, photography, gifts, theater, and LARP events. Every bill is carefully inspected before shipping.

Our Manufacturer

Our bills are made in Peru using an intaglio press, unlike low-quality bills made on inkjet printers. This ensures premium detail and authenticity.

Scanner Tested

Our bills pass every scanner except for ATMs. Perfect for film, theater, photography, and all LARP purposes.
